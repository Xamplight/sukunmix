Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

At SukunMix, we value your peace of mind and your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information inside our mobile application.

1. Information Collection & Use

SukunMix does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. Our app is built to work fully offline and local to your device. We do not track your location, your audio choices, or any device identifiers.

2. Third-Party Services

We may use standard mobile advertising platforms (such as Google AdMob) to display advertisements. These third-party services may collect anonymous identifiers to personalize ad experience according to their respective privacy policies.

3. Local Data Storage

Any customized sound mixes or sleep timer preferences you save inside SukunMix are stored locally on your device using sandbox storage. This data never leaves your phone.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, feel free to reach out to us at sukunmix.app@gmail.com.
