At SukunMix, we believe your privacy is as important as your sleep. We are committed to maintaining your trust by being transparent about the data we handle.

1. Data Collection & Minimization

SukunMix is designed with a "privacy-first" philosophy. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information. The app functions primarily offline. Your personalized sleep settings, volume levels, and mix configurations are stored locally on your device's secure sandbox storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

2. Third-Party Services & Advertising

To keep SukunMix free for everyone, we use third-party advertising services (specifically Google AdMob). These services may collect and use anonymous device identifiers (such as your Advertising ID) to show relevant ads. This data is processed according to their respective privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: View Google Privacy Policy

3. Analytics & Performance

We may use non-identifiable, aggregated technical logs to identify app crashes or major bugs. This helps us ensure that your relaxation experience is never interrupted. No personal user data is ever tied to these logs.

4. Children's Privacy

SukunMix is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our service. We encourage you to check this page periodically for updates.
